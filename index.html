About

Frontend Simplified is an online coding boot camp designed to get beginners into six-figure frontend developer jobs,
offering intensive training in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, supported by AI, expert mentorship, a large community (Discord), and a job guarantee. It's known for practical, project-based learning, self-paced modules, and strong career services (resume/interview prep), with many students finding success quickly after the 12-week program, though some find the initial cost significant.
